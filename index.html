8 Metrics Analysis System

Unlike simple price alerts, our bot analyzes multiple data points to filter out false signals and identify real opportunities.

📊 1. Funding Rate

What it is: The fee that longs pay to shorts (or vice versa) every 8 hours on futures markets.

Why it matters:

  • Negative funding (-0.01% or lower) → Shorts pay longs → Market is oversold → Good for pumps ✅
  • Positive funding (+0.01% or higher) → Longs pay shorts → Market is overbought → Likely correction ⚠️

Example: "Funding: -0.015% (negative → supports growth)"

⚖️ 2. Long/Short Ratio

What it is: The ratio of traders in long positions vs short positions.

Why it matters:

  • Ratio > 2.5 → Too many longs → Likely liquidation cascade ⚠️
  • Ratio 0.5-2.0 → Balanced market → Good for trend continuation ✅
  • Ratio < 0.25 → Crowd is in shorts → Potential short squeeze 🚀

Example: "Long/Short: 1.35 (few longs → good for growth)"

💹 3. CVD (Cumulative Volume Delta)

What it is: The cumulative difference between buy volume and sell volume (updated in real-time via WebSocket).

Why it matters:

  • Positive CVD (+1000+) during price rise → Real demand, strong signal ✅
  • Negative CVD (-1000+) during price rise → Divergence, fake pump ⚠️
  • Strong CVD absorption → Big players accumulating/distributing 🐋

Example: "CVD: +1245 BTC (buyers active)"

📊 4. Volume Analysis

What it detects: Unusual volume patterns that signal distribution or accumulation.

Key patterns:

  • 3x+ volume on highs → "Sold to the crowd" → Likely reversal ⚠️
  • 3x+ volume on lows → Panic selling → Opportunity to buy ✅
  • Normal volume → Trend continuation likely 📈

Example: "⚠️ Giant volumes on highs - likely sold to crowd"

📈 5. Open Interest Divergence

What it is: Comparing price movement with Open Interest (total open positions).

Key signals:

  • Price +10%, OI -10% → Fake pump, positions closing ⚠️
  • OI +30%, price sideways → "Milking phase", big move coming 🎯
  • OI & price rising together → Strong trend ✅

Example: "🚨 Fake pump: OI falling while price rises"

⚡ 6. Liquidations Tracking

What it monitors: Mass forced closures of overleveraged positions (via WebSocket real-time data).

Trading signals:

  • >$1M longs liquidated (SELL) in 15 min → Potential reversal up ✅
  • >$1M shorts liquidated (BUY) in 15 min → Fake pump, likely pullback ⚠️
  • Liquidation clusters → Strong support/resistance levels 📍

Example: "⚡ $2.5M longs liquidated in 15 min → reversal possible"

🔻🔺 7. Historical Minimums & Maximums

What it tracks: All-time lows (ATL) and all-time highs (ATH) for every pair.

Trading opportunities:

  • New ATL → Potential reversal zone, good for long entries 🟢
  • New ATH → Resistance zone, consider profit-taking or shorting 🔴
  • ATH breakout → Possible start of new bull trend 🚀

Independent alerts: /historicalon, /historicaloff, /maxon, /maxoff

⭐ 8. Smart Scoring System

What it does: Combines all 7 metrics into a single 0-8 star rating for each signal.

How it works:

  • ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (8/8) → Perfect signal, all metrics aligned
  • ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5-7/8) → Strong signal, trade with confidence 🟢
  • ⭐⭐⭐ (3-4/8) → Weak signal, trade with caution 🟡
  • ⭐ (0-2/8) → Poor signal, avoid trading 🔴

Example: "Confidence: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/8)"

🎯 LONG/SHORT Recommendations

Based on the Smart Scoring system, the bot provides clear trading recommendations:

  • 🟢 LONG Recommendation → Score ≥4, bullish metrics aligned (negative funding, low L/S ratio, positive CVD, etc.)
  • 🔴 SHORT Recommendation → Score ≥4, bearish metrics aligned (positive funding, high L/S ratio, negative CVD, etc.)
  • 🟡 Weak Signal → Score <4, mixed signals, trade cautiously or wait

FAQ

Where do the bot’s signals come from? 🤔

Our bot analyzes real-time data from Binance Futures, focusing on USDT-M futures. It tracks price movements, volume changes, and market trends to deliver accurate trading signals.

Can I use these signals for spot trading? 💎

Yes! Even though the bot is optimized for Binance Futures, the signals are just as useful for spot trading because price movements on futures and spot markets are closely linked.

Are these signals relevant for other exchanges like Bybit? 📊

Absolutely! Crypto markets are highly interconnected. If a price pumps or dumps on Binance, the same movement usually happens on Bybit, KuCoin, OKX, and other major exchanges.

❓Why do prices stay similar across different platforms?

Thanks to arbitrage trading, price gaps between exchanges get closed almost instantly. Large traders and automated bots buy low on one platform and sell high on another, keeping prices nearly identical everywhere. Understanding Crypto Arbitrage Trading

How can I use these signals effectively?

You can apply them to futures trading, spot trading, or even arbitrage strategies. Since Binance is the largest crypto exchange, its price movements set the trend for the entire market.

Do I need a Binance account to benefit from these signals?

No! Even if you trade on another exchange, our bot’s signals help you spot market opportunities and make informed trades. Wherever you trade, you stay ahead of price movements! 🚀
